About us

Wimma Lab is a concept based on the challenge-centered approach. Purpose of the concept is to integrate students into different kinds of research challenges. Various companies can present challenges for students to solve. This year’s employers come from IT-institute of JAMK University of Applied Sciences. In Wimma Lab you can learn important skills for working life, like teamwork, collaboration with companies and problem solving skills. You will meet many people from different companies, whose knowledge can be very beneficial later. Wimma Lab is also known as Summer Factory (2011-2014) and Challenge Factory (2014-2017) and was re-named in summer 2017 to Wimma Lab, giving chance to expand the concept over the world.
